The RetroPunks are a community of 10,000 punks living in the PUNKWRLD, in their own retro-dystopian universe, powered by a blend of the past and the future.

Each punk is uniquely special, and is more than just a PFP NFT, it represents an identity, a key into the PUNKWRLD, a talisman in the NFT world, an alter-ego on the web.

The RetroPunks were inspired by the original CryptoPunks, and created with a vision to sail the seas of the Web3 and NFT world by forming a community of enthusiast members.

The RetroPunks are to be perhaps one of many projects created by ECHO and related to the PUNKWRLD, and so the journey continues...
